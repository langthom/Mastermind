available commands:

  NEW            Starts a new game with the same starter.
  SWITCH         Starts a new game with the opposite starter.
  MOVE a b c d   Performs a human move with colors <a> <b> <c> <d>.
  EVAL b w       Evaluates the human rating of black and white
                 pins <b> and <w> and calculates the next machine move.
  HELP           Displays this message
  QUIT           Exits the game.
